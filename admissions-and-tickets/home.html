About Tickets and Transport

Ferry

Ferries are one of our many options to get to Jurassic Park™. A max amount of 30 people can ride on the same ferry in your party. Our ferry rides are the most cost effective way to get to Jurassic Park™. Our ferries can take you from Puntarenas to our lovely park in about 2 hours and 50 minutes. (Ferry times can change due to weather and seasons)

Helicopter

We take great pride in our dinosaurs and our fantastic helicopter travels here at Jurassic Park™. Our helicopters only take 40 minutes (Depending on weather) and it will let you enjoy the fabulous views of Costa Rica. Helicopters will leave from San Jose and will go straight to Jurassic Park™ and can only hold up to 10 people from a party. This method of transport is one of the fastest methods of all travel to Jurassic Park™ and at a great deal too! So buy today!

Seaplane

Seaplanes are our most luxurious pick out of all our options. Our seaplanes will leave Los Cobanos to Jurassic Park™ at set times. The trip will take 2 hours and 30 minutes (Depending on weather), but the views are well worth it. You are able to fit 15 people from one party on our private seaplanes. Snacks and drinks will be available free of charge. So buy a seaplane ticket for you or your family today! **Snacks and drinks will be free of charge upon purchase only**

Tickets and Time

When buying tickets, specify how many people are coming and their age groups. Please note that children and senior citizens come at a discount along with military personnel. Select the days/weeks/month you will be staying (Max amount of time is one month. Specify arrival date). (Military IDs will need to be shown at transport locations and at entrance of main park)

Add an Expedition

Here in Jurassic Park’s™ Travel and Admission Department (JPTAD), we can help you include an expedition at a discount if you bundle the tickets and travel. The expeditions that are available in this bundle are Hike to a Volcano and Ride a Triceratops. To learn more, visit Expeditions.
